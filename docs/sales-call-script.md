# Sales Call Script

This is the default guided sales call script for ORI CRUIT HUB.

Use it for:

- first discovery calls
- demo follow-up calls
- pricing calls

## 1. Opening

Use:

`Thanks for taking the time. I want to understand how your team currently handles candidates, documents, legal follow-up, and arrivals, and then show whether ORI CRUIT HUB is a fit.`

## 2. Discovery questions

Ask:

1. how are you managing candidates today
2. where do documents live right now
3. what gets blocked most often between recruiters, legal, and logistics
4. how many people touch the same candidate flow
5. what happens when a passport, PESEL, or permit detail is wrong or late

## 3. Pain reframing

Use:

`What I usually see is not just a software gap. It is an operational control problem: too many documents, too many side channels, and too little visibility across the team.`

## 4. Demo bridge

Use:

`Let me show you how we centralize that workflow in one operating flow instead of Excel, WhatsApp, and internal back-and-forth.`

## 5. Demo highlight line

Use:

`Document upload and OCR-assisted review are active in production. Operators still keep the final review step where scan quality or compliance requires it.`

## 6. Qualification checkpoint

Ask:

- if this worked, who on your team would own adoption
- would you want to test this in a guided pilot with real workflow

## 7. Pricing transition

Use:

`We do not sell this like a cheap generic tool. We run a guided pilot first, so your team validates the workflow in production conditions before broader rollout.`

## 8. Pricing line

Use:

`Our standard guided pilot is EUR 2,500 setup plus EUR 2,000 for a 30-day pilot cycle. After validation, rollout starts from EUR 1,250 per month depending on operator count and scope.`

## 9. If price resistance appears

Use:

`Before lowering price, I prefer to narrow scope. That keeps the pilot serious and lets both sides validate real usage without pretending the rollout is broader than it is.`

## 10. Close

Use:

`If the fit is real, the next step is a guided pilot with a named owner on your side, one defined workflow, and a 30-day validation cycle.`
