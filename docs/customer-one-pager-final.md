# ORI CRUIT HUB

## From WhatsApp and Excel to operational control

ORI CRUIT HUB helps international recruitment agencies run candidates,
documents, legal follow-up, and logistics in one operating flow.

## The problem

Many agencies still manage critical workflow through:

- Excel
- WhatsApp
- email
- shared folders
- side conversations

That creates:

- document chaos
- repeated manual work
- weak traceability
- unclear legal readiness
- poor logistics coordination

## The solution

ORI CRUIT HUB centralizes the operational flow in one system:

- candidate tracking
- direct document upload
- OCR-assisted document review
- manual validation where needed
- legal follow-up visibility
- logistics and arrival coordination

## Why it matters

The value is not only speed.

The value is control:

- fewer broken handoffs
- less document chasing
- better cross-team visibility
- cleaner execution as volume grows
