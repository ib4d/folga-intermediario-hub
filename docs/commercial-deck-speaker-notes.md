# Commercial Deck Speaker Notes

Use this document while presenting the commercial deck live.

The goal is not to read slides. The goal is to narrate them cleanly.

## Slide 1 - Cover

### Message

`ORI CRUIT HUB is not another generic recruitment tool. It is an operating system for agencies handling international recruitment with real document, legal, and logistics complexity.`

### Keep to

- 20-30 seconds

## Slide 2 - The problem

### Message

`The main problem is not only sourcing. The real breakdown happens when candidate workflow lives across Excel, WhatsApp, email, folders, and side conversations.`

### Point to emphasize

- the issue is operational fragmentation
- not just missing software features

## Slide 3 - The system

### Message

`ORI CRUIT HUB centralizes the operating layer: candidates, documents, OCR-assisted review, legal follow-up, and logistics.`

### Point to emphasize

- one operating flow
- not multiple disconnected tools

## Slide 4 - Operational outcomes

### Message

`What changes for the team is visibility, traceability, and fewer broken handoffs.`

### Point to emphasize

- less chasing
- less retyping
- cleaner execution

## Slide 5 - Commercial value

### Message

`The value is not only speed. The value is control. Better control means fewer mistakes, less chaos, and better execution as volume grows.`

### Point to emphasize

- this is why pricing is not commodity-level

## Slide 6 - Current product position

### Message

`The platform is already live in production. Document upload and OCR-assisted review are active. Operators still keep the final review step where needed.`

### Point to emphasize

- honest maturity
- no overclaiming

## Slide 7 - Commercial model

### Message

`We start with a guided pilot because that lets the customer validate the workflow with their real team before broader rollout.`

### Point to emphasize

- guided pilot first
- rollout after validation
- serious, not casual trial behavior

## Slide 8 - Next step

### Message

`If the fit is real, the next step is a guided pilot with one owner, one defined workflow, and a short validation cycle.`

### Point to emphasize

- keep the close simple
- move toward next action, not abstract interest

## Presentation rules

Do:

- move slide by slide
- keep the narrative operational
- keep the pricing slide calm and direct
- answer objections after the deck, not inside every slide

Do not:

- over-explain implementation
- over-promise OCR
- soften pricing before the fit is established
