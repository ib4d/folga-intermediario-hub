# ORI CRUIT HUB - Customer One-Pager

## What it is

ORI CRUIT HUB is an operating system for international recruitment agencies.

It replaces fragmented Excel, WhatsApp, and side-channel follow-up with one
workflow for:

- candidates
- documents
- legal follow-up
- logistics coordination

## What problem it solves

Many agencies lose time and control because the same candidate workflow is
split across chats, spreadsheets, and disconnected follow-up.

That creates:

- document chaos
- weak traceability
- repeated manual work
- unclear legal readiness
- poor logistics coordination

## What the platform gives you

- one place for candidate tracking
- direct document upload
- OCR-assisted document review
- manual validation where scan quality requires it
- legal and logistics visibility in the same operating flow

## Current operating model
