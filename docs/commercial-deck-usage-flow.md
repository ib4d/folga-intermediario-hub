# Commercial Deck Usage Flow

Use this document to decide when and how to send or present the deck.

## 1. When to use the deck live

Use the deck live when:

- the prospect is already on a call
- the workflow pain is real enough
- you need to move from problem discussion to commercial shape

Best use cases:

- guided demo follow-up
- discovery-to-demo transition
- pricing conversation

## 2. When to send the deck after the call

Send the deck after the call when:

- the prospect understood the pain
- the account looks commercially serious
- you want a short recap with structure

Do not send it too early to cold prospects who have not engaged yet.

## 3. Suggested flow in a live call

Use this sequence:

1. 5-10 minutes discovery
2. short workflow explanation
3. deck slides 1-6
4. commercial model slide
5. next-step slide
6. discussion

## 4. Suggested flow after a demo

Send:

- deck
- one-pager
- short proposal if the account is warm enough

## 5. What to pair with the deck

For a warm prospect:

- `docs/customer-one-pager-final.md`
- `docs/short-commercial-proposal.md`

For an active pricing conversation:

- `docs/pricing-deck-guided-motion.md`
- `docs/pricing-policy-guided-launch.md`

## 6. What not to send together too early

Do not send in the first cold touch:

- full pricing policy
- negotiation logic
- long internal runbooks

## 7. Recommended current rule

Use:

`Deck for structure, one-pager for recap, proposal for real opportunity.`
