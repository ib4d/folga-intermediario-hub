# Pricing & Monetization Strategy

## Product Tiers

### 1. FREE (Evaluación)
- **Target**: Individual recruiters or very small agencies testing the waters.
- **Price**: $0 / month.
- **Limits**: 25 candidates total, 10 OCR extractions/month.

### 2. STARTER (Crecimiento)
- **Target**: Boutique agencies starting to scale.
- **Price**: ~$49 / month.
- **Limits**: 250 candidates, 100 OCR/month.

### 3. PRO (Profesional)
- **Target**: Established agencies with constant volume.
- **Price**: ~$149 / month.
- **Limits**: 2500 candidates, 1000 OCR/month.

### 4. BUSINESS (Escala)
- **Target**: Large agencies with multiple offices and logistics teams.
- **Price**: ~$399 / month.
- **Limits**: 10000 candidates, 5000 OCR/month.

### 5. ENTERPRISE (Corporativo)
- **Target**: Multinational recruitment operations.
- **Price**: Custom / Quoted.
- **Limits**: Unlimited candidates and OCR. Dedicated support.

## Revenue Streams
1. **Subscriptions**: Monthly/Annual recurring revenue based on tiers.
2. **Add-ons**: 
   - Extra OCR packs (e.g., $20 for 100 extra extractions).
   - Extra storage for high-res documents.
   - White-label custom domains.

## Value Proposition
- **Efficiency**: Replace Excel/WhatsApp, saving 5-10 hours/week per recruiter.
- **Accuracy**: Reduce data entry errors by 90% using OCR.
- **Compliance**: Built-in GDPR and legal tracking for foreign workers.
- **Logistics**: Single source of truth for arrivals, reducing "no-shows".
