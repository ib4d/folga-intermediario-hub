# Demo Script: Folga Hub SaaS

Objective: show how Folga Hub reduces operational chaos in international
recruitment in 10-15 minutes.

## 1. Opening: the problem

- Hook question: "How much time does your team lose looking for documents in
  WhatsApp or correcting spreadsheet mistakes?"
- Context: "International recruitment becomes legal and logistical chaos very
  quickly. Folga Hub exists to centralize that flow from first contact to
  arrival."

## 2. Dashboard overview

- Action: show the main dashboard.
- Talking point: "Here you can see the operational picture without stitching
  together WhatsApp, Excel, and internal follow-up messages."

## 3. Candidate intake flow

- Action: show candidate creation or public registration.
- Talking point: "You can create the candidate internally or send a public
  intake link so the candidate uploads documents directly."

## 4. Document workflow and OCR

- Action: upload a passport and open OCR review.
- Talking point: "The system extracts useful fields automatically and keeps the
  operator in control through a review step."
- Approved wow line: "You get speed from OCR, but still keep a clean manual
  confirmation step where scan quality needs it."

## 5. Legal and logistics control

- Legal: "Legal sees the cases that require attention, not every side
  conversation."
- Logistics: "Operations can coordinate arrivals and readiness from one panel."

## 6. Export and reporting

- Action: show XLSX export.
- Talking point: "You can generate a clean operational export without manually
  rebuilding the data."

## 7. Close

- Summary: "Folga Hub gives agencies one system for candidates, documents,
  legal follow-up, and logistics."
- CTA: "Would you like a guided pilot or a sandbox demo for your own team?"
