This is the outreach-ready version of the first Tier A accounts.

It is designed to move from "target company" to "usable first-touch plan"
without inventing contacts that are not publicly visible.

## Rule

Where an official site exposes a named public business contact, it is included.

Where it does not, use the official business channel and look separately for:

- founder / owner
- operations director
- sales director
- business development lead
