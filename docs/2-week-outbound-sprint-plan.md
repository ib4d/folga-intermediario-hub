# 2-Week Outbound Sprint Plan

This is the first execution sprint for ORI CRUIT HUB commercial outreach.

It is designed to move from preparation into disciplined market contact.

## Sprint objective

In 2 weeks, achieve:

- first outbound sent to all 8 Tier A accounts
- first follow-up cycle started
- at least 3 meaningful replies
- at least 2 discovery or demo conversations booked

## Operating rule

Quality over volume.

The goal is not to send the highest number of messages. The goal is to create
real movement in high-fit accounts.

## Minimum success criteria

At the end of 2 weeks, at least one of these should be true:

- 3+ replies
- 2+ discovery/demo meetings booked
- 1 clear pilot-qualifiable opportunity

If not, do not panic. Review:

- opening lines
- target roles
- account fit
- whether the pain hypothesis is too generic

## Daily discipline

Every day:

- update CRM status
- set next action date
- record replies immediately
- do not leave accounts without an owner or next step

## Weekly review questions

At the end of week 2, answer:

- which accounts reacted
- which roles reacted
- which message angle worked best
- where friction appeared
- whether pricing came up too early
- which accounts should move into discovery/demo priority

## Decision after sprint

After 2 weeks:

- double down on responsive accounts
- rotate message angle for non-responsive accounts
- deprioritize weak-fit accounts
- prepare demo/discovery notes for booked calls

## Recommended current decision

Use:

`Run the first 2 weeks as a disciplined learning sprint, not as a brute-force campaign.`
