# Commercial Deck Design Spec

This document turns the commercial deck into a presentation-ready structure.

It is meant to reduce interpretation work when converting the content into:

- PDF
- Slides
- Canva deck

## Brand direction

Use the current palette:

- primary accent: `#fcba04` (amber flame)
- background light: `#fbf9ff`
- support neutral: `#f3f3f3`
- text dark: `#0b0500`
- muted support text: `#8a817c`

## General presentation rules

- keep slides visually clean
- use one strong message per slide
- avoid long paragraphs
- use short bullets
- keep pricing slides simple and explicit
- avoid promising fully automated OCR or self-serve SaaS maturity

## Slide-by-slide spec

## Slide 1 - Cover

### Goal

Establish positioning immediately.

### Headline

`ORI CRUIT HUB`

### Subheadline

`Operational control for international recruitment agencies`

### Visual direction

- clean background
- one bold accent block in amber
- minimal layout
- optional supporting subtitle in muted grey

## Slide 2 - The problem

### Goal

Make the operational pain obvious.

### Headline

`International recruitment breaks down when workflow lives across too many tools`

### Content

- Excel
- WhatsApp
- email
- shared folders
- disconnected follow-up

### Footer line

`The result is document chaos, weak visibility, and repeated manual work.`

### Visual direction

- use a fragmented layout motif
- or a simple before-state diagram

## Slide 3 - The system

### Goal

Show that ORI CRUIT HUB is the operating layer.

### Headline

`One operating flow for candidates, documents, legal follow-up, and logistics`

### Content

- candidates
- documents
- OCR-assisted review
- legal follow-up
- logistics coordination

### Visual direction

- simple 5-block flow
- avoid technical clutter

## Slide 4 - What changes operationally

### Goal

Translate features into team-level outcomes.

### Headline

`What changes for the team`

### Content

- cleaner candidate flow
- better document traceability
- legal status visibility
- logistics readiness visibility
- less manual chasing

### Visual direction

- side-by-side before / after
- or 5 short benefit tiles

## Slide 5 - Why this matters

### Goal

Move from operational pain to commercial value.

### Headline

`The value is not only speed. The value is control.`

### Content

- fewer broken handoffs
- less document chasing
- better cross-team visibility
- cleaner execution as volume grows

### Visual direction

- keep this slide sparse
- use one strong highlighted sentence

## Slide 6 - Current product position

### Goal

Set accurate expectations.

### Headline

`Live in production. Ready for guided pilots.`

### Content

- production-deployed
- document upload active
- OCR-assisted review active
- manual validation remains where needed

### Visual direction

- trust slide
- simple badges or checklist style

## Slide 7 - Commercial model

### Goal

Explain how engagement starts.

### Headline

`Start with a guided pilot. Roll out after validation.`

### Content

Standard guided pilot:

- `EUR 2,500` setup
- `EUR 2,000 / 30 days`
- up to 5 operators

Rollout starts from:

- `EUR 1,250 / month`

### Visual direction

- pricing card format
- no clutter
- one primary highlighted pilot card

## Slide 8 - Next step

### Goal

Make the CTA explicit.

### Headline

`Recommended next step`

### Content

`Run a guided pilot, validate the workflow with your team, and move into paid rollout with a defined operating scope.`

### Visual direction

- large CTA slide
- one short closing line
- optional contact details footer

## Suggested final deck order

Use exactly this order:

1. cover
2. problem
3. system
4. operational outcomes
5. commercial value
6. current product position
7. commercial model
8. next step

## What to avoid visually

Do not:

- overload slides with paragraphs
- show too many screenshots on one slide
- add technical implementation details
- make the pricing slide look negotiable by design
- make the deck feel like cheap SMB SaaS

## Recommended current use

Use this deck for:

- guided demo follow-up
- pricing conversations
- pilot proposal support
- PDF send-after-call material
