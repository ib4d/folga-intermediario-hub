# Pilot Commercial Narrative

This document defines the current approved commercial story for guided demos,
pilot conversations, and public-facing CTA decisions.

It is deliberately narrower than a future self-serve SaaS story.

## Current commercial position

Use this sentence internally:

`Private production ready. Distribution readiness in progress.`

Use this sentence with prospects:

`ORI CRUIT HUB is live in production and ready for guided pilots, with OCR-assisted document review and manual validation built into the workflow.`

## What we are selling right now

We are selling:

- guided demos
- guided pilot onboarding
- supported operator rollout

We are not yet selling:

- fully no-touch self-serve onboarding
- fully productized self-serve billing
- zero-review OCR automation promises

## Core story

The core story should stay consistent:

1. agencies outgrow Excel and WhatsApp
2. candidates, documents, legal, and logistics must live in one operating flow
3. OCR helps operators move faster
4. manual review remains available where scan quality or compliance requires it
5. the platform is already deployed and operational in production

## Approved CTA hierarchy

Use this order of intent:

1. `Start free` / `Create workspace`
   - best when the user is ready to try the product flow
   - routes into onboarding
2. `See the demo`
   - best when the user needs guided understanding first
   - routes into the demo page and sandbox path
3. `Request demo`
   - best for higher-touch or enterprise conversations
   - should stay associated with guided follow-up, not blind self-serve

## Approved claims

These are approved:

- "Replace fragmented Excel and WhatsApp candidate operations."
- "Run candidates, documents, legal follow-up, and logistics in one system."
- "Document upload and OCR-assisted review are active in production."
- "Manual correction is built into the workflow."
- "The production deployment, backups, SMTP, and cron routines are already in place."

## Claims to avoid

Do not claim:

- "Fully automatic passport OCR with no manual verification"
- "Completely frictionless onboarding for every entry path"
- "Fully productized self-serve SaaS billing rollout"
- "Broad mass-market distribution readiness"

## Short scripts by channel

### Landing page

Primary promise:

`From WhatsApp and Excel to total operational control.`

Primary CTA:

`Start free`

Secondary CTA:

`See the demo`

### Guided demo

Opening line:

`This is the operating flow for agencies handling international recruitment across candidates, documents, legal review, and arrivals.`

OCR line:

`OCR speeds up document handling, and operators keep the final review step where needed.`

### Pilot conversation

Pilot offer:

`We can onboard you into a guided pilot with your team, your workflow, and a controlled rollout path instead of asking you to adopt everything cold.`

### Pricing discussion

Pricing line:

`For this stage, pricing should be presented as a guided commercial rollout, not yet as a fully hands-off self-serve motion.`

## Exit condition for this narrative

This narrative remains the approved one until all of the following are true:

- self-serve billing is fully productized
- public onboarding is consistently low-friction
- OCR quality matches the promise being made
- monitoring and alerting maturity go beyond the current lightweight uptime baseline
