## Slide 1 - Title

ORI CRUIT HUB  
Guided Pilot and Rollout Pricing

Subtitle:

`Operational control for international recruitment agencies.`

## Slide 2 - The problem

Agencies lose time and control because the candidate workflow is fragmented
across:

- Excel
- WhatsApp
- email
- side conversations

Result:

- document chaos
- unclear legal readiness
- weak logistics coordination
- repeated manual work

## Slide 3 - What the customer is buying

ORI CRUIT HUB is not a generic CRM.

The customer is buying:

- one operating flow for candidates, documents, legal, and logistics
- OCR-assisted document handling
- manual validation where needed
- operational visibility
- guided rollout instead of abandoned self-serve

## Slide 4 - Why pricing is premium

This is priced above cheap SMB SaaS because:

- it replaces multi-step operational friction
- it reduces document handling chaos
- it supports cross-functional coordination
- it is sold with guided rollout and onboarding

Positioning line:

`Price like a specialized operational system, not like a commodity tool.`
